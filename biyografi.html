Biography

Aleaddin Agbulut, one of veterans in cooking world was born at 1961. After he noticed his interest in cooking, he began to work at local patisseria. At the age of 18, he began to work at a bigger patisseria as a waiter. After 2 years working, now he began to work at Izmir Kristal Restaurant. Then he moved to Hotel Goldensand at Didim, because reputation of it. After working 5 years at Hotel Goldensand, he decided to move to a bigger place, Hotel Grant Plaza, Izmir. After 4 years, because of some relocation problems, he moved to other places, called Asansor.

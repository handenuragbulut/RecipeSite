Chicken Roasting

Materials

  • 250gr Hungarian Salami
  • 300gr cucumber pickle
  • A little jar of Kapari
  • A jar of mayonnaise

Description

Boiled potatoes, peas and carrots are thoroughly filtered and then added to the cheese, bacon and salami and mixed. Yoghurt and mayonnaise are mixed and added to the mixture. After being sprinkled in salt, mixed and left in the cupboard for half an hour, then served.
